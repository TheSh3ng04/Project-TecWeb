Rules


Each player has n pieces. Your pieces will be disposal on the bottom row.

Dice roll: The value of the dice is obtained by the number of sticks with the light side facing up.

Name Light up Value Extra trow
Sitteh 0 6 ✔
Tâb 1 1 ✔
Itneyn 2 2 -
Teláteh 3 3 -
Arba'ah 4 4 ✔


A piece's first move must be a roll of 1 (Tâb).

Pieces move in the direction shown on the board. A piece may enter the top row only once, and only if all of the player's pieces have left the starting row. A piece may not return to the starting row.

A piece cannot move to a square occupied by another piece of the same player. If an opponent's piece occupies the destination square, it is captured and removed from play.

The game ends when one player has captured all of the opponent's pieces.
